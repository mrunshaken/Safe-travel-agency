About Peace Travel

Redefining Luxury Travel Since 2010

Peace Travel Agency is the premier luxury travel consultancy based in Hargeisa, offering bespoke travel experiences for discerning clients worldwide. Our expert team crafts personalized journeys that exceed expectations.

Located in the heart of Hargeisa near Masque Ali Matan, Quruxiba Building, Floor 2, we combine local expertise with global connections to create unforgettable travel moments.

Our philosophy is simple: every journey should be seamless, extraordinary, and tailored specifically to your preferences. From private jet charters to exclusive villa stays, we handle every detail so you can focus on creating memories.
